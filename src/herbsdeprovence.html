Herbs de Provence The brand Herbs de provence originated in the south of France in Provence and was inspired by the free and wild nature. Our select herbal oils lavender, camomile and thyme are considered universal healing oils. The purer the oil is, the greater its effect. In order to maintain this quality, we only use pesticide-free, organic herbs that are sustainably grown, produced and packed. The specially developed spray form cross-links the face with the right dose of oil. The fresh scent has a renewing, harmonising and calming effect on body, mind and soul.
